Chicken quesadilla recipe

Melt-in-your-mouth chicken quesadilla!

An easy and delicious chicken quesadilla for anyone who wants something to snack on!

Steps

  1. 1 pound chicken breasts (cut into bite sized pieces)
  2. 1 tablespoon oil
  3. 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  4. 1/2 cup diced onion
  5. 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  6. 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  7. 2 cups shredded melting cheese
  8. 4 medium flour tortillas
